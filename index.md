## Overview

Surf Connect is a web application that address the problem where UH students from outside of Hawaii have trouble trying to surf. Surf Connect will make this easier for these students by recommending surf locations based on their surfing ability and location. Surf Connect will also connect students together with similar surfing ability and similar times they would like to surf. By doing this, Surf Connect addresses the overall issue by providing a location for students to surf, connect with others to have fun, and provide details on surf conditions to ensure saftey.
